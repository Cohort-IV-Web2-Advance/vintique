Who We Are

More Than a
Vintage Shop

Vintique is a modern e-commerce platform dedicated to bringing timeless fashion back to life. We specialize in carefully curated classic vintage shirts that combine elegance, character, and authenticity.

Vintique was proudly developed by a team of Web2 Advanced Students from Blockfuse Labs, combining innovative web technologies with a passion for digital commerce. This project represents not only our technical expertise but also our commitment to building scalable, user-friendly, and secure online platforms.

Our Mission

Beauty Deserves
a Second Life

We believe the most sustainable object is the one already made. By giving vintage pieces a second life, we reduce waste, honour craftsmanship, and keep history alive.

Authenticity First

Every item is inspected by our team of experts before listing. We test, research provenance, and document each piece with a full authenticity certificate.

Sustainable Commerce

Choosing vintage is choosing the planet. Every purchase extends the life of a beautiful object and reduces demand for new production — good for collectors and the earth alike.

Community of Collectors

We've built more than a marketplace — a community of 12,000+ passionate collectors who share knowledge, recommendations, and a love for things that endure.

Expert Restoration

Our in-house conservators carefully clean, stabilise, and when needed, restore pieces to wearable or displayable condition — without erasing their beautiful history.

Fair & Transparent Pricing

No auction anxiety. Every piece is priced fairly based on condition, rarity, and provenance — with detailed descriptions so you always know exactly what you're buying.

Loved, Not Just Listed

We only list items we genuinely believe in. If a piece doesn't meet our standards of quality, beauty, or authenticity, it doesn't make it onto Vintique — full stop.
