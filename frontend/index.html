Our Story

Preserving the
Beauty of the Past

Founded in 1991 by antique enthusiast Margaret Holloway, Vintique began as a small market stall in London's Portobello Road. Today, we curate hundreds of authenticated vintage items from estate sales, auctions, and private collections across Europe and West Africa.

Every piece is hand-selected, professionally cleaned, and given a detailed provenance report. We believe vintage shopping should be trustworthy, joyful, and sustainable.
