What Unites Us

Our Shared Values

Genuine Passion

We only hire people who are genuinely moved by beautiful, old things. You cannot fake the care we put in.

Rigorous Honesty

We describe every flaw. Trust is the foundation of everything we do.

Deep Knowledge

Degrees in art history, material science, fashion, and conservation. We know what we're talking about.

Global Perspective

Twelve nationalities, six time zones, one shared love. Our diversity makes our curation richer.

Work With Us

Passionate About
the Past?

We're always on the lookout for people who share our obsession. Whether you're a conservator, a photographer, a seasoned buyer, or a brilliant writer, if vintage objects genuinely move you, we want to hear from you.

Vintique is a small, close-knit team. We don't hire often, but when we do, we look for curiosity, integrity, and a genuine love of things built to last.
